Hi! I’m a professor at UFRPE. Previously I have been a post-doc researcher at UPE - Brazil. I did my Ph.D. in Computer Sciences at UFPE - Brazil, under supervision from Jaelson Castro. During my Ph.D there were two research periods abroad: 10 months at UPC - Spain, supervised by Xavier Franch; 1 year at UniTn - Italy, under John Mylopoulos.

Besides researching on requirements engineering, autonomic systems and everything software engineering, I’ve worked developing Digital TV software at CESAR. I’m also experienced as a web developer, being world finalist of the SCORE contest and developing the brazilian website Tuga - Filmes da TV (discontinued).
